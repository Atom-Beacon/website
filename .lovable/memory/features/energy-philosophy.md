---
name: Energy-inclusive philosophy
description: No negativity toward any energy source — fossil fuels, renewables, or nuclear all valued
type: preference
---
Atom Beacon does NOT discriminate against any energy source. ALL forms of energy — coal, oil, natural gas, solar, wind, hydro, geothermal, nuclear — are respected and valued.

Key rules:
- Never use "intermittent" to describe solar/wind — use "variable" if needed, or just describe their strengths
- Acknowledge fossil fuels' historical contributions positively (built the modern world)
- Don't frame any energy source as "the opponent" or enemy
- Focus is nuclear, but other sources "have their place" and "have a purpose"
- "Energy isn't a competition. It's a portfolio."
- Comparisons should be factual and neutral, not framed to make other sources look bad
