## Why visit

**Atom Beacon** is built around a simple idea: nuclear energy belongs in an honest discussion about how we power the world. The site is a trusted place to stay informed, learn from the ground up, and read opinions that treat nuclear as one part of a **diverse energy portfolio** — not a cage match with every other source.

The tone is deliberate: advocates for nuclear, **without tearing down** coal, gas, solar, wind, hydro, or geothermal. The homepage puts it plainly: *energy isn’t a competition — it’s a portfolio*, and every source that keeps the lights on has a role.
